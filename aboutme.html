Who Are We?

SAFE&SOUND is a music collective created with the
intention of uplifting women and nonbinary people in
the music industry and to keep abusers out of shows.

Founded by Carson Kloeck and Isabel Zweig in August 2021,
SAFE&SOUND has been featured in the New Orleans Gambit
and Ra Ra Rebel Zine. SAFE&SOUND's debut show took place
on October 16th, 2021 and had a turnout of over 100 attendees.
